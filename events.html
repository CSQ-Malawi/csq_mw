JOIN THE CAUSE WITH CSQ

Walkathon

The CSQ Annual Walkathon is held as a commemoration of the World cancer day, on the day or near the date which is 4th February. The event was started in 2018 and has continue to happen every year and grow in numbers gradually. The event has the objective to promote hope to those that are battling cancer, it’s a statement that there are people out there that care. The walkathon also has the objective of telling the nation that cancer survivors are not broken vessels, but are determined, strong and passionate people and that survivors are as human as everyone else. We also use the event as a means of fund raising and currently we are using the event to raise funds for the purchase of land and construction of a cancer home in Blantyre that will cater for patients in Blantyre.

CSQ Dinner

Annually funds allowing CSQ holds a fundraising dinner where we raise hope and funds and promote awareness on cancer and cancer related issues.
