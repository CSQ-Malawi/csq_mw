Welcome to Cancers Survivors Quest!

Malawi is a small country located in the Central part of Africa in the tropical region. Cancer in Malawi is increasing at a very high rate, currently there are over 10,000 new cases that are being diagnosed. Cancer Survivors Quest CSQ is one of the organisations that was established in 2011 as a platform to promote the fight against cancer. In Malawi communities still lack awareness about the condition and is handled as a personal issue. We dont have adequate medical personnel and facilities to treat it; recently the first cancer centre was opened in 2025 by the Malawi Government, and another International Blantyre Cancer Centre IBCC privately owned.

Mission Statement

The mission statement of the Network shall be to aid in the promotion and fight against Cancer through awareness. Enshrined in this is the principle of enhancing the lives of persons with Cancer and Cancer survivors by providing basic needs throughout the country through raising funds using various fundraising techniques.

Objectives of CSQ

The CSQ has many objectives which in time may change, but the network has core objectives and these are:

  • To create a Network comprising of individuals, groups and organisations that collectively work towards enhancing the lives of Cancer patients, parents and cancer survivors.
  • To create and run, 20 clubs and/or support groups by 2030, through awareness and fundraising that will promote and co-ordinate a platform for Cancer and its related issues.
  • To promote, support and complement government policies and programmes that fall under non-communicable diseases (NCD's), including cancer, directed at persons with cancer, parents and survivors.
  • To increase engagement and participation of people with lived experience in all levels of decision making.

Our Virtues in the Cancer Fight

It's not easy to experience Cancer...be it from a personal perspective or through a loved one, but once you are diagnosed or affected by cancer, you are will never be the same.

Voluntarism

At Cancer Survivors Quest we are all volunteers dedicating our time, resources’ and energy to support cancer patients. This is one way we believe we can pay back to society.

Love

All our efforts are done out of love, we love our country, our people and we share this love to all citizens of Malawi especially cancer patients and survivors.

Perseverance

Fighting against cancer is not simple nor easy so we teach perseverance to cancer patients, survivors and care givers. We also persevere in our work as we support the patients as we believe in not giving up on any patient.

Hope

A very important help in any fight including cancer is hope, hope is very important; with hope it allows healing and hope is contagious and can spread like wildfire.
